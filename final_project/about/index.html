About Us

A brief overview of our mission:

The mission of troubleshoot.io is to provide simple to follow, step-by- step solutions to help novice and experienced computer users solve common computer hardware, network, and software-related issues that they may see on a day-to-day basis.

Site Goals

  • Help users resolve issues promptly and with easy-to-follow technical guides
  • Provide clear documenation so they aren't frusturated or scramling for other sources
  • Organization of information into categories for convenience
  • Allow users to find all infomration they need in one location so they can learn and understand the cause of the computer-related issues they are having

Intended Audience

  • Age Range: 16-65 years old (Students, Working Professionals, Small business owners, Older adults)
  • Tech ability: Beginner to moderate computer users; sometimes, advanced computer users
  • Common Wants or Needs: Step-by-step Documentation or guides, self-service, all information obtained through a single source without seeking out other sources
  • Location: Global (accessible to all); Primarily, English speakers as the website has only the English translation
