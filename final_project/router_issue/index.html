Router Issue

Solution to Router Issue:

Steps

  • Step 1: Restart your router
  • Step 2: Check if the issue is Wi-Fi or internet (test wired vs wireless)
  • Step 3: Fix slow speeds (optimize placement, reduce interference, change band/channel)
  • Step 4: Fix no internet (test Ethernet, reset router, contact ISP if needed)
  • Step 5: Check for missing Wi-Fi network (placement, overheating, reset name)
  • Step 6: Fix devices that won't connect (restart, forget/reconnect network)
  • Step 7: Fix frequent disconnections (reduce interference, update firmware, replace router)
  • Step 8: Fix smartphone connection issues (restart, reset settings, reconnect)
  • Step 9: Try advanced fixes (settings, updates, password, full reset)
  • Step 10: Contact your ISP if the problem continues
